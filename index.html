Holy Cow Halal Spot

Wonderful spot for Halal and impossible burgers. Had the impossible burger, beef slider and the largest onion rings I have possibly seen in my life. Everything was fresh and delicious and the sauces provided were unique and complimented the food very well. - Hyder Ahmed

Check it out! 

The Halal Guys

We wanted the line from our hotel window for three days. Today we finally joined in. Oh my goodness...worth the wait! It was delicious! We got a gyro & hummus. We say that we would be fine with that being a daily meal the rest of our days! So delicious! - Brooke S.

Check it out! 

Sami's Kabab House

Soooo good! My new favorite spot for Halal. Ordered the Chicken Over Rice which was the perfect amount of food, packed with deliciousness. The chicken was so juicy and all the sauce mixed together was an explosion of flavor in my mouth. Will definitely be back! - Josh B.
